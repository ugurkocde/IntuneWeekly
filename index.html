About

Most Intune newsletters look backwards: they summarise what already shipped. Intune Weekly leads with what is coming - Roadmap items moving to GA, Message Center actions with deadlines, and in-development features - so admins know what to plan around before it lands in their tenant. Real-world community signal from r/Intune and Tech Community sits alongside the official sources, because Microsoft does not publicly acknowledge every issue that admins hit.
